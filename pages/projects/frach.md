**Frach Collective** was a self-organized, grassroots volunteer collective that supported people on the move in the Una-Sana Canton of Bosnia and Herzegovina from December 2020 through autumn 2022. It was co-founded together with the [KochKollektiv](https://wemakeit.com/users/kochkollektiv), a Zurich-based action kitchen that normally cooks for demonstrations and climate camps. With their existing infrastructure, network, and fundraising experience they helped get the project off the ground quickly - the first group was on the ground in Bihać within weeks of the [Lipa Camp fire](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lipa_camp) in December 2020, a month earlier than originally planned.

I took part in the initial organization, and two months present in donation runs, on-the-ground distribution of non-food items and administering medical aid in Bihać and the surrounding area.

## What We Did

Operating out of Bihać, a bottleneck city on the Balkan route near the Croatian EU border, the collective:

- Distributed food, sleeping bags, tents, clothing, shoes, hygiene supplies, medicine, SIM cards, and firewood to people living in informal settlements and jungle camps around Bihać and Velika Kladuša
- Maintained direct contact with people on the move (POM) and responded to their needs after violent pushbacks by Croatian police
- Coordinated with other NGOs and local partners, including eventually handing organizational continuity to [On the move e.V.](https://www.on-the-move.org/) (Tübingen) in mid-2021
- Continued operations for two full years, far beyond the originally planned two months
